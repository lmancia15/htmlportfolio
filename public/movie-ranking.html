The Best Three Movies According to Lesley

3 Movies that sit as my top favorites.

Battle Royale

I am a big fan of gore horror and this movie kept me hooked with it's enticing story and amazing special effects.

A Silent Voice

Both a heartwarming but also heartbreaking anime that focuses on the deeper struggles people may face alone and with others.

Pearl

A beautiful execution of country horror with phenomenal acting as well as relatable characters.
